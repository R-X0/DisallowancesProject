3. Camarillo Resolution No. 2020‑63 – “Temporary Outdoor Dining Program”

   • Summary: Recognizing that indoor dining was severely limited by health orders, the City of Camarillo waived zoning requirements and fees for restaurants to set up temporary outdoor dining areas. This proactive step allowed restaurants to serve customers outdoors, thereby mitigating losses that would otherwise occur under strict indoor-only limitations. For Q3 2020, when many indoor venues remained closed or at limited capacity, this measure provided a critical lifeline to local eateries.

Such actions contributed to a broader effort to alleviate the economic pressures on businesses during the challenging third quarter of 2020.
